Jakub is a research scientist at Columbia.

Jakub's research combines bits of computer science with operations research and behavioral economics. At Columbia, he works with Christian Kroer and Garud Iyengar. He holds a PhD from NTU, advised by Bo An and Allan Zhang. During his PhD, Jakub interned at UChicago, hosted by Haifeng Xu.
