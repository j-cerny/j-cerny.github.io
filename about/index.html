About Me

I am a PhD Candidate at SCSE, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. My research is funded by ASTAR SINGA scholarship.

Research Interests

Artificial intelligence, multi-agent systems, computational game theory, decision making, bounded rationality, robustness, optimization, cooperation

Education

  • MSc summa cum laude in Artificial Intelligence (major) and Robotics (minor) at FEE, CTU in Prague
  • MSc in Discrete Models and Algorithms at Department of Applied Mathematics, FMP, Charles University
